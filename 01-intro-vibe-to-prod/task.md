## 🛠️ Assignment 01: The Architect's Foundation

Welcome to your first challenge in the Full-Stack Journey! In episode 01, we talked about the difference between "Vibe Coding" a messy prototype and "Engineering" a production-ready application. Before we start building features, you need to prove you can set up a rock-solid foundation.

Complete the following quiz, then submit your work in the dedicated Discord channel(#task-assignments-fsvcpr)!

### 🧠 Part 1: The Mindset Check (Quiz)

Answer these three questions briefly in your own words.

- `The Monolith Trap`: We discussed how AI often generates a "monolith." From an engineering perspective, why is putting your UI, state, and API calls all in one massive file dangerous for production?

- `Vibe Coding`: How do you think of vibe coding? How are you using it today? What are good and bad things of vibe coding you have discovered so far?

- `Production Ready`: Have you ever pushed your app to production that real users are using? What kind of problems have you faced?
